About EasyGrocery

Discover a Seamless Shopping Experience!
User and Seller/Store Manager Profiles Easily create separate profiles for users and sellers, unlocking unique dashboards tailored to your needs.
Effortless Category Management: Sellers can create item categories and add multiple items with essential details like item name, price, unit, and manufacturer information.
Insightful Summary Page: Sellers can access a comprehensive summary page, complete with a category-wise bar graph showcasing available item numbers and a line graph illustrating the quantity of items sold.
Complete Profile Visibility: Sellers can view their profiles, along with a detailed list of sold items, customer details, payment history, and item availability status.
Real-Time Stock Updates:Stay informed with the latest stock status! If an item's quantity reaches zero, it will display an "Out of Stock" badge. Sellers can easily edit and update available quantities.
Seamless Login and Logout: Both customers and sellers can enjoy secure login and logout functionalities using sessions.


Explore by Categories: Upon logging in, customers can browse through a plethora of category-wise items or use the search box to find products by name, category, price, manufacture date, or seller.
Detailed Item Descriptions: Click on any item card to view comprehensive details and make informed choices.
Effortless Ordering: Add items to the cart with just one click, or manually enter the desired quantity. Proceed to checkout, confirm your order, and find it conveniently listed under "My Orders" for future reference.
Personalized Profile: Customers can view their profile details, providing an enhanced user experience.
